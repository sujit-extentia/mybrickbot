MyBrickBot for Alexa

Get captivated playing LEGO®? Do you want to be so engrossed that you forget about anything else?

Well then, welcome to the world of MyBrickBot! A space where Alexa on Amazon Echo meets LEGO®. Fans can do everything from finding available sets and themes to managing their Brickset.com accounts.

Love LEGO®. Play LEGO®. Here’s your one-stop shop for Alexa users!

Read More Download Skill

FEATURES

  • Read out all sets available in a user's wish list and own collection
  • Add and remove sets from a user’s Brickset.com account
  • Read out specifications about any particular set
  • Read out names of all the themes available on Brickset.com
  • Provide sub-themes for users’ existing themes
  • Send error messages to unregistered users
