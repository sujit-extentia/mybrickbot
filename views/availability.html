Availability

Immediate on the Alexa Skills directory. To set up and install this skill, search for it by name at http://echo.amazon.com (in the 'Skills' section) after signing in with an Amazon account. After clicking on the ‘Enable Skill’ button, users have to log in with their Brickset.com credentials. Users who are not registered with Brickset.com can enable the skill anonymously.
